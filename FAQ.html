Frequently Asked Questions – Purchasing

How can I buy a car from CarHub?

You can browse available cars on our website and complete the purchase online or schedule a visit to one of our locations.

Do you offer installment payments?

Yes, we provide monthly installment options. Just choose "Pay in Installments" during checkout.

Is there a warranty on purchased cars?

All our cars include a minimum 6-month warranty. Extended plans are also available.

Do you offer car delivery to my location?

Yes, we provide home delivery for most locations across the country. Delivery fees may apply based on your distance from our hubs.
